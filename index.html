About

Since 1869 Beau’s Bonbons has created beautiful tasty treats to style your table and dance across your taste buds. The store first opened when Beau, the original owner and creator made his first piece of candy. A chewy taffy that melts in your mouth and our most popular item available. Since then Beau’s only grew. We hired more people, created more candy, and made more people happy. We stand for making healthy treats for everyone to enjoy, whether you are young in life, or young at heart. Our standards never change, we will continue to pride our selves on natural ingredients and using the healthiest options available. We keep people happy and healthy. Enjoy a variety of our original recipes and add some sweetness to someone’s day.
